Reflection Questions

  1. How did you feel that the patient wasn’t ready to quit using drugs despite having an abscess that required hospitalization?
  2. What other harm reduction education tips would you provide for the patient?
  3. What stage of change does the patient appear to be in at this time?
    How would you educate the patient if they were one stage prior? One stage later?
  4. How would you respond to your colleague if they stated "When is bed 3 getting out of here? We don’t need any more addicts in here they are so time consuming to deal with and are just frequent fliers?"
  5. What trauma informed principles would be used during this interaction?
